About the shop

A neighbourhood counter for real vehicle problems.

Taisho Lanka (Pvt) Ltd trades as a dealer in all kinds of motor spare parts, tyres and batteries, right on the Dummalasuriya roadside at No. 54, Sri Lanka 60260. It's the kind of shop where you describe the noise your vehicle is making and walk out with the part.

The shop serves cars, vans, three-wheelers and motorcycles used across Dummalasuriya, Madampe, Chilaw and the wider Kurunegala district — everyday running repairs, tyre changes and battery replacements without needing to travel into a bigger town.
