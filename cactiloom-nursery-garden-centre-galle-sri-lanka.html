FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Cactiloom's plants and services.

What types of plants does Cactiloom sell?

We stock a wide range: cacti (from tiny globulars to tall columnar species), succulents in dozens of varieties, indoor tropical plants like Monstera and Pothos, flowering outdoor plants, and rare collector specimens.

Do you offer wedding plant giveaways?

Absolutely — it's one of our specialities! We create bespoke wedding favour packages with mini potted cacti and succulents, custom pots, ribbons, and personalised tags. We've served 50+ weddings across Sri Lanka. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your needs.

Can you deliver island-wide in Sri Lanka?

Yes. We carefully pack all plants for safe transit and deliver across Sri Lanka. Contact us at +94 71 337 4279 to discuss delivery costs and timing for your location.

How do I care for a cactus or succulent?

Cacti and succulents thrive in bright indirect light, need watering only when the soil is completely dry, and prefer well-draining soil. We provide a care guide with every purchase and our WhatsApp line is open for follow-up advice.

Can I visit the garden centre in Galle?

Yes! We're open 7 days a week from 8 AM to 7 PM in Galle. Call us at +94 71 337 4279 for the exact address and directions.

Do you do bulk/wholesale orders?

Yes, we welcome bulk orders for weddings, corporate gifting, events, and landscaping projects. Enquire on WhatsApp for pricing and lead times.
