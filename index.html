About Amithafz

Sri Lanka's Home for Cakes & Baking Supplies

Amithafz FoodnTasty has been serving Sri Lanka since 2017, providing premium quality cakes, cupcakes, food packaging, and baking supplies to individuals, home bakers, restaurants, and event planners island-wide.

Based in Colombo and operating across the country, we specialize in wedding cakes, birthday cupcakes, gift boxes, and all the packaging materials you need to present your creations beautifully.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver across Sri Lanka?
Yes! We deliver island-wide including Colombo, Kandy, Galle, Negombo, Kurunegala, Jaffna, Kalutara and all major cities. Call 0729622034 to arrange your delivery.
What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale?
Minimum order quantities (MOQ) vary by product. Most packaging items have MOQ of 100 units for wholesale pricing. Contact us for custom bulk quotes.
How do I place a custom cake order?
WhatsApp or call us at 0729622034 with your requirements — size, flavor, design, occasion, and date. We'll confirm availability and pricing for your custom order.
Are prices negotiable for large orders?
Absolutely! We offer special wholesale pricing for large orders. Please note prices shown are subject to change. Contact us directly for current pricing and bulk discounts.
Do you offer baking classes?
Yes! We offer online birthday and wedding cupcake baking classes. Check our shop for class details and registration.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery and bank transfers. Contact us via WhatsApp for payment details when placing your order.
